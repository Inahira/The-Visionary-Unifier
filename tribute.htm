A Tribute To Dr. Abdul Salaam

BIOGRAPHY

On the Jan 29, 1926, Chaudhry Muhammad Hussein and Bibi Hajira Hussien had a baby boy at their two-bedroom abode in Jhang.

They named him Abdus Salam — ‘servant of peace’.

At the age of 12, Abdus Salam was admitted to Jhang’s local college for his intermediate education.
